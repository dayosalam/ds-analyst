Data Analyst at AIESEC UAE

  • Technologies: Python, Excel, Google Sheets, Looker Studio
  • Project date: 09 September, 2023

Customer

AIESEC UAE plays a vital role in fostering youth development within the country by providing these international exchange opportunities, alongside initiatives organized by its local committees in Dubai and Sharjah.

Challenge

AIESEC UAE, a leading youth leadership organization, aimed to improve the effectiveness of their international exchange programs. They lacked a data-driven approach to understand participant trends, program preferences, and areas for improvement.

Solution

Collaborated with other data analyst to partake in a data analysis project to gain valuable insights and optimize AIESEC UAE's programs. Here's what I did

  • Competitor Analysis
  • Data Gathering & Strategy
  • Dashboard Design
  • Data Protection Strategy

Results

Data insights allowed for a more tailored program matching process, connecting participants with the most suitable opportunities based on their skills and interests. Improved program design and targeted marketing strategies led to a noticeable increase in participant applications. Data-driven insights helped AIESEC UAE identify areas for program improvement, leading to a more positive participant experience. AIESEC UAE gained the ability to make informed decisions regarding future program development and resource allocation based on solid data analysis.

Technologies and Tools

Python, Excel, Google Sheets, Looker Studio
