Data Analytics Consulting for StockX Sneaker Sales Analysis and Forecasting

  • Technologies: SQL, Python, Tableau
  • Project date: 09 April, 2022

Customer

StockX is a leading online marketplace for buying and selling authentic sneakers, streetwear, and collectibles. They cater to a passionate community of sneaker enthusiasts and resellers.

Challenge

StockX was facing challenges in keeping up with the ever-changing sneaker market trends and accurately forecasting future sales. They sought to leverage data analytics to gain deeper insights into their customer base, optimize pricing strategies, and improve sales forecasting accuracy.

Solution

I started with the analysis of the StockX business objectives and requirements for the future sales forecasting analysis. After that, I reviewed the customer base, pricing strategies, and sales. Then I suggested enhanced data-driven insights to optimize their business strategies.

  • Data Acquisition and Integration
  • Data Preprocessing
  • Exploratory Data Analysis to investigate data for patterns and anomalies, test hypothesis and check assumptions.
  • Sales Forecasting with Statistical Modeling
  • Data Visualization and Reporting

Results

The data analytics consulting empowered StockX to significantly improve their business strategies. Advanced statistical models enhanced their ability to forecast market trends, leading to optimized inventory management and reduced risk of overstocking or understocking popular sneakers. Additionally, data-driven insights allowed StockX to develop effective pricing strategies and gain deeper customer understanding. This enabled them to tailor marketing efforts to specific customer segments, potentially boosting sales and profitability.

Technologies and Tools

SQL, Pandas, Python, NumPy, Jupyter, Tableau.
